    • ABOUT US

    Welcome to our Online Library Management System, a brainchild of the talented team from Bengal College of Engineering and Technology pursuing BTech in Computer Science and Engineering. As aspiring software engineers in our 5th semester, we embarked on this journey to create an innovative solution for efficient library management. Fueled by our passion for technology and knowledge, we crafted a user-friendly platform that simplifies the way libraries operate and users access resources. With dedication and expertise, we've designed a seamless experience for students, researchers, and educators. Our goal is to enhance accessibility, promote learning, and foster a love for reading in the digital age. Join us in revolutionizing the way libraries function and let's explore the world of books together. Thank you for being a part of our journey.
